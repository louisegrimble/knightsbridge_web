About Us

The Knightsbridge Strategic Group is dedicated to ensuring both business leaders and governments across the globe have expert geopolitical insights and strategic planning in place, allowing them to thrive in a challenging landscape.

Our Services

1. Scenario Testing & Wargaming

Be it the Russian invasion of Ukraine, KSG provides scenario wargames to allow clients to strategize for challenging situations on the horizon, and set in motion mitigations before that scenario ever arises. Our wargaming team can help develop a strategy to handle adversity.

2. Geopolitical Intelligence Reports

Our members have access to a vast range of geopolitical intelligence reports prepared by our intelligence team on the biggest challenges in geoeconomics, inter-state rivalry for key resources, energy supply, conflict, and much more.

3. Tailored Geopolitical Analysis

Our analysts are keen to work with you to specifically assess geopolitical threats and opportunities facing your company, government and/or industry.
