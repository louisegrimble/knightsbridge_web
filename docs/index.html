About Us

The Knightsbridge Strategic Group is dedicated to ensuring both business leaders and governments across the globe have expert geopolitical insights and strategic planning in place, allowing them to thrive in a challenging landscape.

Our Services

1. Scenario Testing & Wargaming - Be it the Russian invasion of Ukraine, The October 7 Hamas attack on Israel, or the long-term rise of China, unprepared companies and governments across the globe have been left scrambling to deal with the impacts. KSG provides scenario wargames to allow clients to strategize for challenging situations on the horizon, and set in motion mitigations before that scenario ever arises. Be it supply chain concerns in the Red Sea, A Chinese invasion of Taiwan, or a critical infrastructure attack, our wargaming team can help develop a strategy to handle adversity.

2. Geopolitical Intelligence Reports - Our members have access to a vast range of geopolitical intelligence reports prepared by our intelligence team on the biggest challenges in geoeconomics, inter-state rivalry for key resources, energy supply, conflict, and much more.

3. Tailored Geopolitical Analysis - Our analysts are keen to work with you to specifically assess geopolitical threats and opportunities facing your company, government and/or industry.
